League of Legends Arcane - Jinx Edition (2021)

In 2021, I developed and edited my first video using Adobe After Effects, using the "Arcane" series from Netflix as the theme for this project. I selected the scenes with a focus on a character I strongly identify with, Jinx/Powder.

League of Legends Worlds 2024 - Fan Edit (2024)

In 2024, I created a fan video edit that captures the essence of the "Worlds" championship in League of Legends using DaVinci Resolve. As a passionate League of Legends fan, I aimed to highlight the skill and strategy of the players, as well as the electrifying atmosphere of the tournament. This project not only showcases my editing skills but also my deep appreciation for the game's competitive scene. The video serves as a tribute to the players, teams, and fans who make the "Worlds" championship a pinnacle of esports entertainment.

Demon Slayer - Fan Edit (2024)

For this project, I created a fan video edit inspired by the popular anime series "Demon Slayer." I wanted to capture the intense battles and stunning visuals that make this anime so captivating. To bring out the energy and emotion, I chose rock/pop music to create dynamic and aggressive takes. I had a lot of fun exploring different editing techniques to make the scenes come alive.

Use mask carefully! (2023)

After COVID in 2021, I was asked to create a meme video about using masks. I decided to make it extra special by animating it frame by frame with my own illustrations. The project humorously highlights the challenges of eating while wearing two masks, blending a bit of humor with an important message about being careful.
